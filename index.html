25/03/19-31/03/19


We realised it was worth mentioning that when events are grouped together (in a box) it means you can easily walk between the shows in one evening. The order makes the walk easy.


You can also click on the address of a show to open google maps or the time of an event to save it to your calendar.


Week overview

Monday - Central - Three openings in Mayfair, including children’s art show at the Nehru Centre and four female Italian artists group show at Repetto.

Tuesday - Central - Crosstown - Talks at Mosaic Rooms [Earl’s Court], 50 Golborne [Westbourne Park], Gazelli [Mayfair] and Bosse & Baum [Peckham].

Wednesday - East - Juno Calypso talk at the Sarabande Foundation [start of fantastic set of talks in April including Grayson Perry and Yinka Shonibare].

Thursday - West - Two shows to walk to in Notting Hill at Serena Morton and Daniel Benjamin. Central - Neat cluster of openings in Mayfair/Soho, with shows at Frith Street [actually on Golden Sq, Soho] and Parafin, then two openings that will go later at Aindrea Contemporary and Julius Getty. Can also take a curator tour [6:30-7:30] of the Deutsche Borse Prize at the Photographer’s Gallery. East - over on City Island in Canning Town opening at Arebyte curated by Most Dismal Swamp, great name, great space.

Friday - Central - Sobering and proactive sustainability talk [6:30] at the LSE from Mike Berners Lee discussing, There is no Planet B: A Handbook for the Make or Break Years. South East - In Bermondsey openings at Matt’s Gallery and Shipment’s space in the Biscuit Factory, and also at Arts Hub and Gossamer Fog in Deptford.

Saturday - West - Talk [3-4] from assistant curator Natalia Grabowska on Emma Kunz at The Serpentine, it's a good show, check it out. Central - Could start day with brunch [11-1] at Dellasposa for their current group show, The Future is Female. At the Photographers Gallery, great looking talk [3-4] from Deutsche Borse Prize displaying artist Arwed Messmer. North - Curator tour [4-5] of the various shows at the Zabludowicz Collection by Tiffany Zabludowicz herself.
