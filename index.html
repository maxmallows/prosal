Week 19 - 11/03/19-17/03/19

First things first, if around in the day 10am-6pm, head to Piano Nobile in Holland Park for Leon Kossoff, A London Life.

Monday - Central - Excellent looking panel discussions at Alan Cristea [Pall Mall] and Hauser & Wirth [Savile Row] examining their current shows Protest and Remembrance and Geta Brătescu’s The Power of the Line.

Tuesday - Central - Pre-auction display at Cork Street Galleries of work produced from pencils & crayons salvaged from the Calais Jungle, prior to charity sale in April at Phillips. South - Interesting looking show for Ghizlane Sahli at Sulger Buel in Southwark. East - Later on, BF Artist Film Festival starts at SET in Dalston, 7-10.

Wednesday - Central - Shows for Dean Levin at Pippy Houldsworth and Ruben Alterio at Osborne Samuel, can visit before or after a talk at 6:30 at Vic Miro looking at their current Danish group show. South - The Auction Collective display work at Menier Gallery nr London Bridge, prior the auction itself on Saturday. East - Opening for Ed Fornieles at Carlos Ishikawa in Stepney.

Thursday - West - Nr Baker Street Robert Janitz opens at König. Nice. Central - Walking south to north can start at opening for Aboudia at Jack Bell in Mason’s Yard, over to Dean Street for Bedwyr Campbell at Southard Reid and then Delio Jasse at Tiwani. North - In Chalk Farm, the Zabludowicz Collection opens 3 new shows. East - Clusters of shows around in Hoxton and Bethnal Green, standouts being 3 artist show at L'étrangère examining the anthropocene, Thomas Yeomans at Xero, Kline & Coma, and the launch of a new augmented reality art app, Kairos², at Space Hackney.

Friday - West - Opening for Emma Kanz at the Serpentine, if weathers dry head down. South East - Three shows in Bermondsey at Ugly Duck, the V-23 space at the Biscuit Factory and Sugarhouse Studios.

Saturday - South - Talk on Alan Turing at Studio Voltaire in Clapham, closing performance for Libita Clayton at Gasworks in Oval, and The Auction Collective have their auction starting at midday.

Sunny skies forecast. Enjoy.
