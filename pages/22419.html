Week overview

Monday - East - Doc on the early career of Brian Eno on show at Moth Club, and opening at Stour Space in Hackney Wick.

Tuesday - West - Panel for current Asian artist moving show at Elephant West. East - Elizabeth Neilson of the Zabludowicz & David Hoyland of Seventeen Gallery provide a talk [7-9] on How to Price Your Art at the Sarabande Foundation. South East - In Camberwell opening for Victor Seaward at Lily Brooke.

Wednesday - Central - Painting show for Denzil Forrester covering the UK dub and reggae scene of the 1980’s, with walkthrough [6:15-35] from Melanie Keen of Iniva. Also tour [6:30-7:30] of Alice Anderson’s current show at Waddington Cutsot. East - Raven Row host talk [6:30-8:30] on methods to fundraise in the arts by Ianna Nitsou of Chisenhale Gallery. South East - Performance [7-8] from installation artist Ivan Riches at the South London Gallery.

Thursday - West - Two openings round Latymer Road/Ladbroke Grove at Unit 1 and Muse Gallery. Central - Chantal Joffe is in conversation [6:30-8] at Vic Miro. 11 openings across Mayfair, St James’, Soho and Fitzrovia - main highlights being Cy Twombly at Bastian, mid C20th abrastaction shows at Olivier Malingue and Lévy Gorvy, Brian McLean at Bernard Jacobson and a UAL show at Koppel Projects. The Original Print Fair also opens at the RA and is open till 9. East - 8 openings from Shoreditch to Cambridge Heath - highlights are a dual opening for Lola Walker + Maxine Walker at Autograph, all day opening for large group show at Protein Studios looking at patriarchy and environmentalism, then along Hackney Road shows at Buday, Annka Kultys and IMT. Further afield, Mandy El-Sayegh is in conversation [7-9] discussing her current exhibition and practice at Chisenhale, and interesting looking show from Felicity Hammond at Arebyte in Canning Town. South East - 2 openings in Deptford at Creekside Artists and APT.

Friday - Central - Early evening events at each of the major institutions with talks at Tate Britain, Royal Academy, National Gallery and Tate Late. South East - 3 openings in Deptford a few minutes from each other at Deptford Does Art, The Change Room and Seager Gallery, and Dulwich Picture Gallery is open till 10pm.

Saturday - West - Curator Katy Watson hosts a walking tour [12-1] of Hito Steyerl at The Serpentine. North - Programme Director Maitreyi Meheshwari tours [3-4] round Rachel Rossin’s AV installation at the Zabludowicz Collection. East - Two openings round London Fields at Playground and also a one night show for Art House.

Sunday - South East - Screenings of films by Tracey Emin at Five Bells in New Cross.
